Pronunciation Guide

A quick-reference for reading Hebrew letters and vowel marks (nikud) used throughout this dictionary.

Hebrew Alphabet (אָלֶף-בֵּית)

Letter Name Transliteration Pronunciation Example
א Aleph ʾ Silent / glottal stop אֱלֹהִים (Elohim)
בּ / ב Bet / Vet b / v "b" as in boy / "v" as in vine בֵּן (ben – son)
גּ / ג Gimel g "g" as in good גּוֹי (goy – nation)
דּ / ד Dalet d "d" as in door דָּם (dam – blood)
ה He h "h" as in house הַר (har – mountain)
ו Vav v / w "v" as in vine וָו (vav – hook)
ז Zayin z "z" as in zero זָהָב (zahav – gold)
ח Khet ḥ Guttural "ch" (like Bach) חַיִּים (chayyim – life)
ט Tet ṭ "t" (emphatic) טוֹב (tov – good)
י Yod y "y" as in yes יוֹם (yom – day)
כּ / כ / ך Kaf / Khaf k / kh "k" as in king / "kh" as in Bach כֹּהֵן (kohen – priest)
ל Lamed l "l" as in love לֵב (lev – heart)
מ / ם Mem m "m" as in man מֶלֶךְ (melekh – king)
נ / ן Nun n "n" as in name נָבִיא (navi – prophet)
ס Samekh s "s" as in sun סֵפֶר (sefer – book)
ע Ayin ʿ Deep guttural (pharyngeal) עוֹלָם (olam – world/eternity)
פּ / פ / ף Pe / Fe p / f "p" as in peace / "f" as in faith פֶּה (peh – mouth)
צ / ץ Tsadi ts / ṣ "ts" as in its צַדִּיק (tsaddiq – righteous)
ק Qof q "k" (back of throat) קָדוֹשׁ (qadosh – holy)
ר Resh r Rolled "r" רוּחַ (ruach – spirit/wind)
שׁ / שׂ Shin / Sin sh / ś "sh" as in shalom / "s" as in sun שָׁלוֹם (shalom – peace)
תּ / ת Tav t "t" as in truth תּוֹרָה (Torah – law/teaching)
